About Duncan Backus

Duncan was called to the Bar in 1990 and worked in-house in the interactive games industry in both a legal and business affairs capacity for, amongst others, Electronic Arts Inc. (as Senior Lawyer, Europe) and Empire Interactive Europe Ltd (as General Counsel).

Setting up Backus Law LLP in 2009, Duncan now provides legal and business advice, and solutions, to developers, publishers, distributors and investors in the interactive games industry, software developers and sports organisations.
